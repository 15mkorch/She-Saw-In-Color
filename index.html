The Grey

Unseen to humanity lies a co-existing world where a force called the shedim moves like a shadow — whispering, seeping, deceiving. One by one, they pull a veil over human eyes until the world bleeds of life, of warmth, and meaning.

"They often moved like shadows, their whispers seeping in before they're even noticed."

The Color

The camu are guardians to the humans, unseen to them, yet woven into every breath, moment, and choice. They've dedicated their lives to protecting their color, but as humanity falls into the grey, their fight grows desperate. Now, the camu must battle harder than ever to protect the living force that gives life to the world.

"To see in color is an act of defiance."

Kailani is seventeen, a star student from Indiana who sees the world richer, more alive, more meaningful than anyone around her. She's been quietly building a meticulous plan to escape her chaos and become the person she's been hiding. What she doesn't know is that she's the last one left — and the key to saving everything.

For fans of Ted Dekker · For fans of Jandy Nelson · Dual POV · YA Fantasy

Meet the Characters

Joseph

The Ancient Guardian

Joseph is an ancient guardian assigned to protect Kailani from birth. He didn't expect to be carrying the burden of a mission so big that everything he knows depends on it. Not only must he confront the darkness closing in on Kailani, but as he learns more about his enemy, he must wrestle with his past to save the future.

Kailani

The Last Seer

Kailani is seventeen and from Green Grove, Indiana. She has spent her whole life hiding in the background, quietly architecting a meticulous plan to escape the life she was born into. Beneath the surface, she carries more secrets than anyone around her knows. What she doesn't know is the biggest of all.

Artibak

Agent of the Grey

Artibak moves like shadow: patient, precise, and impossible to predict. He's highly ranked among the shedim and feared for his unmatched ability to pull humanity into the grey. His tactics perplex both the camu who fight against him and the shedim who fight beside him, leaving everyone with the same uneasy question: what is he really after?

Will she step into the light? Or let the grey win?
About the Author

Mary Korch

Mary Korch writes stories that sit at the intersection of the seen and unseen — where ordinary life brushes up against something ancient and urgent. She Saw in Color is her debut novel.

She believes the world is far more vivid than most of us dare to notice, and she writes for the ones still willing to look.
